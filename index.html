What is SmallTown Steam?

SmallTown Steam was created by a group of high school students passionate about providing students the opportunity to explore STEAM subjects (science, technology, engineering, arts, and math). We offer many resources to help students embark on their own unique STEAM journey.

‎ ‎ ‎

Our Classes

We have conducted multiple sessions teaching both elementary and middle schoolers about all aspects of STEAM. This teaching sessions are typically about an hour each, with a session every day for a week.

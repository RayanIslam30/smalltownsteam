Who are we?

We are a group of high school students passionate about STEAM and making sure students had the opportunity to explore science, technology, engineering, art, and math and discover their interests in these topics. We offer many resources to help students explore STEAM.

Our Classes

We have conducted multiple sessions teaching both elementary and middle schoolers about all aspects of STEAM. This teaching sessions are typically about an hour each, with a session every day for a week.

Art Gallery

While thinking of the best way to incorporate the A in STEAM, we came up with the idea of an art gallery! We will add more artwork into the gallery as we get more artwork to show.
